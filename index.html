About

Jessica is passionate about helping people take control of their personal and professional lives. She is fascinated by what makes people tick and she will give you the tools so that you can shine, thrive, love your life, love your work.

She founded, built and sold multiple businesses. She then went on to become management consultant and innovation strategist where she worked with Fortune 500 executives to develop new products and services as well as helped organizations learn how to foster creativity and nurture more innovative corporate cultures. Jessica has delivered speeches, workshops and coaching for clients including Coca Cola, Pfizer, American Express, Hasbro, Johnson and Johnson, LVMH, and Hearst among many others. She has spoken to audiences of hundreds of people throughout the world.

She now brings her expertise to audiences using a unique approach helping people capture unlikely connections, leading to fresh thinking and disruptive ideas. Everyone will learn practical skills on how to actually come up with new ideas, how to embody the behaviors that foster creativity, and the practical frameworks to implement those ideas that can be applied immediately.
